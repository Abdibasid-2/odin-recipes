Eggplant lasagna

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 medium eggplants, sliced into rounds
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for frying

Eggplant Lasagna Recipe Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Fry the eggplant slices until golden brown on both sides. Drain excess oil on a paper towel.
  4. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  5. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, half of the shredded mozzarella, half of the grated Parmesan, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  6. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of marinara sauce.
  7. Arrange 3 lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
  8. Place a layer of fried eggplant slices on top of the noodles.
  9. Spread a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture over the eggplant.
  10. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of marinara sauce.
  11. Sprinkle the remaining shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan on top.
  12. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  13. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  14. Let it cool slightly before serving.

Eggplant Parmesan Lasagna is a delightful twist on the classic dish. It features layers of crispy fried eggplant slices sandwiched between lasagna noodles. Each layer is generously spread with a creamy mixture of ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese infused with aromatic herbs. Baked to perfection, it creates a savory and satisfying lasagna with a unique texture and a harmony of flavors.
