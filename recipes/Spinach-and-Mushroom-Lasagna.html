spinach and mushroom lasagna

Description

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Marinara sauce
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Small onion
  • Garlic cloves
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Dried basil
  • Dried oregano
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Spinach and Mushroom Lasagna Steps

  1. Cook 9 lasagna noodles until al dente.
  2. In a skillet, brown 1 pound of ground beef or Italian sausage with chopped onion and minced garlic.
  3. Stir in 2 cups of marinara sauce, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix 2 cups of ricotta cheese with half of the shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan.
  5. Layer the meat sauce, noodles, and cheese mixture in a baking dish.
  6. Top with the remaining cheese.
  7. Bake at 375째F (190째C) for 25 minutes covered, then another 10-15 minutes uncovered.
  8. Let the lasagna cool slightly before serving.
